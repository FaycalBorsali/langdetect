Massa-Carrara () is a province in the Tuscany region of Italy. It is named after the two main towns in its territory: Carrara and Massa, its capital.Geography and administrationThe province covers has an area of 1,157 km², and a total population of 200,695 (2005). There are 17 comuni (singular: comune) in the province.
