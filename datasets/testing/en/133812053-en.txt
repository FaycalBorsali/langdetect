Flag ratio: 2:3]]The Flag of the Netherlands Antilles is white, with a horizontal blue stripe in the center, one-third of the flag's hoist, superimposed on a vertical red stripe of the same width, also centered; five white, five-pointed stars are arranged in a pentagon pattern in the center of the blue band, their points up. The five stars represent the five main islands of Bonaire, Curaçao, Saba, Sint Eustatius, and Sint Maarten.  Image:Flag of the Netherlands Antilles (1959-1986).svg|1959-1986: Until Aruba seceded in 1986, it was also represented in the flag. Thus the original flag of the Netherlands Antilles had six stars.
