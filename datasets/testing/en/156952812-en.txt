  Cabeceiras de Basto (pron. ) is a municipality in Portugal with a total area of 241.8 km² and a total population of 17,775 inhabitants.The municipality is composed of 17 parishes, and is located in the district of Braga bordering the municipality of Montalegre to the north, Boticas to the northeast, Ribeira de Pena to the east, Mondim de Basto to the southeast, Celorico de Basto to the south, Fafe to the west and Vieira do Minho to the northeast.The present mayor is Joaquim Almeida Barreto, elected by the Socialist Party.The municipal holiday is September 29.ParishesAbadimAlviteArco de BaúlheBastoBucosCabeceiras de Basto (parish)Cavez (formerly Cavês)FaiaGondiãesOuteiroPainzelaPassosPedraçaRefojos de Basto (Cabeceiras de Basto)Rio DouroVila NuneVilar de CunhasCategory:Towns in Portugal Category:Municipalities of Portugalde:Cabeceiras de Basto es:Cabeceiras de Basto eu:Cabeceiras de Basto fr:Cabeceiras de Basto it:Cabeceiras de Basto nl:Cabeceiras de Basto pl:Cabeceiras de Basto pt:Cabeceiras de Basto ru:Кабесейраш-де-Башту fi:Cabeceiras de Basto tr:Cabeceiras de Basto vo:Cabeceiras de Basto
