He is 5' 11" tall and weighs eleven stones (70 kg). He started his career in the 1998/99 season. He went on to play for ŁKS Łódź and GKS Katowice, and made his debut for Wisła Kraków in April 2001.He has been capped 12 times for Poland national football team, scoring twice. He was selected to the 23-men national squad for the 2006 FIFA World Cup finals held in Germany, where he almost scored a goal against Ecuador in their 2-0 defeat, with an impressive shot that hit the post, following teammate Ireneusz Jeleń's effort hitting the post as well.
