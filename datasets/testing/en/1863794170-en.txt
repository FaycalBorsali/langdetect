Montemor-o-Velho (pron. ) is a town and municipality of the Coimbra district, in Portugal. It has roughly 30,000 inhabitants.SportsMontemor-o-Velho has one of the only international sized 2 km rowing lakes in Portugal. The lake hosted its first rowing race in 2002 (the Coupe de la Jeunesse), before it was completed. During the competition, FISA judges noted that the lake was not wide enough to meet international standards. After then, it was rebuilt to meet these standards.
