        David Neville (born June 1, 1984) a sprinter, a native of Merrillville,Indiana, became the first individual track and field medalist out of Indiana University since Willie May won silver in the 110-meter hurdles in 1960. He won a gold medal in the 4x400m men's relay in the 2008 Summer Olympics.Neville finished with a bronze medal in the men's 400m race with a time of 44.80 secondshttp://www.nbcolympics.com/trackandfield/resultsandschedules/rsc=ATM004100/index.htmlOfficial race results, accessed September 21, 2008.. At the finish line he dove forward on the wet track to win the bronze by 0.04 seconds.While being interviewed, he finished his response with "... to give it my all and, uh, give God the glory."
